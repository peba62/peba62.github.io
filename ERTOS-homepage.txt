General overview

Peter is a Research Leader in the Software Systems Research group. His
research interest is in automated deduction, in particular automated reasoning
in first-order logic and its applications, in particular for business rules
and process analysis.

Research interests 

Advancement of automated deduction, in particular first-order logic theorem
proving; applications for software verification, knowledge representation, and
business rules/process analysis; exploiting connections into related areas such as logic
programming, description logics and nonmonotonic reasoning.
