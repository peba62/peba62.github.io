Syllabus

This course covers logical and computational aspects of both automatic and interactive theorem proving and has a strong practical component. Topics include: resolution and its variants for classical first order reasoning; tableaux-based methods; tactic-based interactive methods for first order and higher order reasoning; reasoning in arithmetical and other theories. Students will be expected to conduct experiments using a variety of contemporary automated reasoning software. Assessment will be largely project-based.
